Teamfight Tactics Project

Currently working on ...

I am currently developing a tactical assistant application for "Teamfight Tactics" (TFT) players, designed to deliver real-time strategic guidance throughout their games. The application is being built to validate player IDs, fetch match histories via the Riot Games API, and offer tailored champion and item recommendations. As the match unfolds, my application will dynamically update its suggestions to help players refine their team compositions and strategic approaches. My goal is to craft a vital resource for TFT enthusiasts aiming to elevate their gameplay experience.
